About WildLiveWatch

WildLiveWatch is a playful way to explore the world’s wildlife. Inspired by the joy of safari animals in a Madagascar sunset, we stream live animal cameras from around the globe and bring them together into one friendly site.

Our Mission

We want to make it fun and easy for families, kids, and curious explorers to connect with nature — whether that’s spotting elephants by a watering hole, watching penguins waddle, or simply enjoying the calm of a forest stream.

How It Works

We aggregate public live streams and present them in a colorful, easy-to-use grid. All videos autoplay muted (to comply with browser rules), and you can unmute any tile to hear the sounds of nature.
